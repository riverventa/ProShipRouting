At ProShipRouting, our story is rooted in decades of real-world maritime experience. Founded by seasoned professionals with backgrounds in vessel operations, ship routing, and weather optimization, we set out to create a service that goes beyond automated solutions—one that provides real-time decision-making, personalized routing, and expert support for every voyage.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to enhance maritime efficiency, safety, and cost-effectiveness by delivering tailored navigation solutions. Recognizing the challenges of unpredictable weather, fuel optimization, and voyage planning, we built ProShipRouting as a service that puts shipowners and charterers first.

From our first routes across the North Atlantic to expanding into Western and Southern Africa, our commitment has remained the same—to provide precise, reliable, and expert-backed routing that keeps vessels on the safest and most efficient path.

Today, ProShipRouting continues to evolve, integrating cutting-edge data, hands-on expertise, and customer-focused solutions to redefine modern maritime navigation.

At ProShipRouting, our mission is to revolutionize maritime navigation by providing precise, real-time routing solutions tailored to the unique needs of every vessel. We believe that efficient voyage planning is not just about optimizing fuel consumption but also about enhancing safety, reducing delays, and ensuring smooth operations in all weather conditions.

Commitment is to deliver expert-driven, data-backed navigation support that empowers shipowners and charterers to make informed decisions. Unlike fully automated systems, we combine advanced technology with human expertise, ensuring that each route is carefully evaluated and adjusted based on real-time conditions.

With a focus on efficiency, safety, and cost-effectiveness, we strive to be a trusted partner for maritime professionals worldwide, helping them navigate the complexities of modern shipping with confidence and precision.

Join us as we redefine maritime routing—ProShipRouting: Precision in Every Voyage.

Industry Insights on Weather Routing

Optimised weather routing improve fuel efficiency, reduce voyage time, and enhances safety

The Nautical Institute

“Proper weather routing helps vessels avoid adverse weather, resulting in lower fuel consumption and more efficient voyages. By carefully planning routes to steer clear of storms and unfavorable conditions, ship operators can optimize both fuel use and operational costs, ultimately boosting profitability and reducing environmental impact.”

Lloyd’s List

“Real-time weather data is crucial for creating optimized routes that avoid severe weather, reducing the risk of damage and delays. This proactive approach to routing ensures smoother voyages, protects cargo, and enhances safety at sea. By integrating weather analytics, ship operators can ensure more predictable and efficient journeys.”

International Maritime Organization

“Advanced meteorological guidance offers vessels the ability to plan voyages with optimal safety and fuel efficiency in mind. By utilizing up-to-date weather information, ships can avoid disruptions, reduce delays, and enhance operational efficiency. This proactive approach supports better decision-making and maximizes performance throughout the entire journey.”
